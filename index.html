For Your Company

Building Relationships
  • Success Metric: Connecting on 2 deeply personal topics with that individual
  • Challenge: How do you build relationships remotely?
    • Focus on the quality of your conversation by intermixing emotional vulnerability and humor
  • Sub Goals
    • Meet the Team
      Goal: understand key people, process, politics, and pain points
      1. Particularly product, design, and engineering
      2. Support, success, sales, marketing
    • Understand Manager Painpoints
      1. Manager's expectations for my role: goals, KPIs
      2. Manager's own goals and KPIs
      3. Confirm 30/60/90
      4. Secure existing product documentation
